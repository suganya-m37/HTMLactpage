vijay

Chandrasekaran Joseph Vijay was born on 22 June 1974 in Madras (now Chennai), Tamil Nadu.[4][5] His father, S. A. Chandrasekhar, is a film director and his mother, Shoba Chandrasekhar, is a playback singer and vocalist.[6] His father is a Christian and his mother is a Hindu.[7][8] Vijay had a sister, Vidhya, who died when she was two years old.[9] Vijay did his schooling initially at Fathima school, Kodambakkam and later at Balalok school, Virugambakkam.[10][11] He pursued a bachelor degree in visual communication from Loyola College, but dropped out early to focus on his acting career.[12]
